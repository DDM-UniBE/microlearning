Who we are

Advancing medicine
through digital intelligence

The Department of Digital Medicine (DDM) at the University of Bern bridges cutting-edge computational research with clinical practice. We develop and evaluate AI tools, data-driven methodologies, and digital health solutions that improve patient outcomes and transform how medicine is taught and practised.

DigiMed Learn is our open micro-learning platform — a curated library of concise video lectures produced by DDM researchers and invited experts. Each session distills complex topics in AI, medical imaging, genomics, and digital health into focused, accessible content for clinicians, researchers, and students.

Our mission is to accelerate the responsible adoption of digital technologies across the health system by making world-class expertise freely available at any time, anywhere.

Our Mission

"We believe that knowledge should have no barriers. DigiMed Learn exists to place the expertise of our researchers directly into the hands of the clinicians, scientists, and students who need it most — in concise, evidence-based formats that fit the rhythm of modern healthcare."
