Who we are

Shaping the Healthcare
of Tomorrow

The Department of Digital Medicine (DDM) is dedicated to shaping the healthcare of tomorrow by transforming innovative ideas into practical healthcare solutions.

DigiMed Micro Learning Platform is our open micro-learning platform, a curated library of concise video lectures produced by DDM researchers and invited experts. Each session distills complex topics in AI, medical imaging, genomics, and digital health into focused, accessible content for clinicians, researchers, and students.

Our mission is to accelerate the responsible adoption of digital technologies across the health system by making world-class expertise freely available at any time, anywhere.
